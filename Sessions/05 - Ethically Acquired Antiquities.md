[[Hazelmere]] left on his own into town and went to the guild hall for the [[Bastion Collegium of Master Engineers|Bastion Collegium]], the [[Maison Forge]]. He approached a gnome clerk doing paperwork at one of the high counters for help. After verifying his Arcaneum scrip, the gnome takes Hazelmere's order for for clockwork parts and metal plates and refers him to the mercantile guild for any other general supplies. Hazelmere went there to buy axes, shovels and ropes.

We left [[Ghilain|Brother Ghilain]]'s home with [[Reb Tevye]] and headed into [[Wycrof]]. At the gate, the guards stopped Reb Tevye and told him that he was responsible for making sure we behave. We otherwise didn't have any trouble entering the city. We first visited the baths on the south side of town, followed by a bowery, then a smithy that Reb Tevye trusts. He and [[Collins de Jarrag Iolaire|Collins]] hired the smithy to do some work on their gear. [[Hank]] asked if he works with any exotic materials; the smithy said that occasionally mithril or old relics pass through, but he mostly just works on creating or maintaining war gear. 

As we left the smithy, we spotted Hazelmere and grouped back up. Reb Tevye told us there's someone he wants us to meet and took us to the [[Laurel Crown Inn]]. We met [[Agathe Brelin]], the innkeeper, who served us some cider and sat with us to talk. She told us about the third cohort marching in from the south, which is marching on double rations and can't get more supplies from the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]]. We asked her about the [[Ember Veil]], to which she replied she knows enough and that we'd find what we needed in the stables. She knew we had a job from Brother Ghilain and gave us a candle to burn for instructions. In the stables, the porter [[Jorren]] told us that we'd need to be at the crossing three bells after midnight.

After we left the stables, Hazelmere spotted a bowery on High Street named [[Harkus Hunt and Haggle]]. We went to Reb Tevye 's home in the Cut, where he burned the candle away from where any guards might see. The candle held a metal rod engraved with the same details on the time and place we had heard from Jorren, as well as that we could request supplies through "A" or "J".

Reb Tevye and Hank headed back to the Laurel Crown Inn to ask her for some explosives. They also asked Jorren to head north of the crossing and fake the trail of a passing [[Iron Consortium|Consortium]] raiding party. They then headed south out of down to do the same. 

We stopped by Harkus Hunt and Haggle before leaving town. A young, absent-minded girl named [[Igrid]] was running the shop. Hazelmere pulled a crownmark from a secret compartment in his boot and handed it to the girl before asking to speak to the owner. After some persuading, she went into the back and eventually an old man named [[Osbert Harkus]] came out. Hazelmere said he was looking for [[Caroline Harkus]]. The man claimed to not know who Hazelmere meant, but told him that he'd "see what he could do." Collins and [[Ghithgod]] saw there were healing potions in the store's stock and Harkus told them he could also sell six hour darkvision injectables for 20 gold. We bought some crossbow bolts then headed out of town to the river crossing.

We met back up with Reb Tevye and Hank there and used the remaining time before the caravan arrived to prepare. We put explosives on the bridge, set a few trees ready to fall down onto the path, and Hazelmere used the supplies he got from the guild hall to build a steel defender.

When the caravan finally arrived, there were six guards leading a horse-drawn cart. Reb Tevye recognized one of the guards as Captain Cenric, a man that had been coming in and out of the citadel in town all day. We blew the bridge just as the caravan reached it, killing the horse and a guard and hurting the others. However, the blast what caught Hank, Hazelmere, and Reb Tevye, injuring them. Collins immediately struck down another, but then the captain turned on Collins and nearly killed him with two heavy blows to the chest. Reb Tevye healed Collins with a spell and we quickly dispatched the rest of the caravan guards without issue. 

Inside the cart we right heavily warded chests, which we put into Hazelmere's bag of holding, along with the guard's weapons, armor, and gold. We also found a sealed envelope and a black rod. Hank saw signs of there being a secret compartment, but didn't manage to find it before we needed to leave.
