After we [[09 - Anachreon Avenged|stepped into the circle]], it activated and sucked us through. We landed in a large room carved out from stone, a tiered amphitheatre with a teleportation circle in the center. Pillars formed a circle around the center of the room, and both the pillars and walls were completely covered in engraved in sigils. The room appeared very similar to the [[Izuri Gate System|gate rooms]] we've seen. There was a prominent inscription above the main entrance to the room that we recognized from the [[Last Saga of The Izuri]] and the [[Gate Almanac]]. People in hooded ceremonial clothes stood all around the room on the top tier of the amphitheater against the walls.

Just after we arrived, the engravings all around the room along the top of the wall began to glow and a bright, warm light lit up the center of the room. It spread to fill the whole room and as it passed over us we felt magically compelled to speak the truth. [[Collins de Jarrag Iolaire|Collins]] reached for an arrow, but [[Hazelmere]] put a hand out to stop him. 

A woman called out to us asking, "Who are you?" Hazelmere replied with his name and said he was here to meet with [[Caroline Harkus]]. [[Ghithgod]] also answered, but Collins and [[Marlamin]] stayed silent. The hooded figures made no move to reply, and eventually Hazelmere answered on behalf of the other two. Then the woman asked who we serve and each of us replied that we only served ourselves, except Marlamin who says the [[Tymoro|Changebringer]]. There were some whispers in the room at Marlamin's reply, then the woman asked why we came here. Hazelmere answered again that we came to meet with Caroline Harkus. She asked what we wanted and Hazelmere replied saying guidance. She asked what we were afraid of. Hazelmere answered by saying he was afraid that a fight would break out between us and them, due to how on edge they were putting Collins and Marlamin.

Finally, the woman took off her hood and revealed herself as Caroline Harkus and told us to come up the steps to talk to her. The rest of the hooded figures left the room, except two who waited in the entrance behind Caroline. After we climbed up the steps to meet her, she said she was surprised to see Hazelmere again, but happy to welcome us all into her childhood home. She asked where [[Hank]] and [[Reb Tevye]] were, though didn't know them by name. After we said they had both gone home, she told us that she was acquainted with [[Brother Ghilain]] and "the rest of them" and knew about recent events in [[Wycrof]]. She expressed sympathy for [[Jorren|Jorren's]] death. Collins asked where we were and she said she would show us, leading us out into the hallway.

It opened into a tunnel that slowly ascended towards the surface, with various rooms and halls branching off of it. We passed by children running and playing and many people just going about daily life. Caroline said we were on the west coast of [[Anchara]], in a town named [[Mystara]]. She also said that the place we were currently in was a "place of remembering" that had been around for centuries. She asked us all to promise to keep it a secret and threatened that she would have to come kill us if we didn't.

Caroline then led us into a study/bedroom, where she had us all take a seat at the table and asked someone outside to bring refreshments. After we all sat down, Collins asked if she could read the text we had seen engraved in the auditorium we arrived in. She confirmed she could, then continued on to say that she is a part of the [[Kithenir]], the last descendents of the [[Izure]]. She said that the Kithenir have been dedicated to remembering and preserving the history of the Izure for 225 generations, though much of it was lost, taken, or destroyed when the empire fell. She told Hazelmere that she took an interest in him because of his research on the gates. She wanted to keep tabs on what the [[Arcaneum]] knew about them through him.

Marlamin asked Caroline if she knew about the [[Fall of the Izure|cataclysm]]. She said that she knew both about that and the [[Heavens Gate]], but nothing else about the gates. She said she went to the Arcaneum two centuries ago to find it what they knew, but could never break into the political structure there. She did find out that the Arcaneum had a secret library somewhere with more information about the Izure, but never found it.

Caroline then asked us to explain how we knew so much about the gates. Hazelmere told her about [[Bridgewater]] and the gate there, as well as how we used it to [[01 - Escape from Bridgewater|travel]] to Wycrof. Caroline was especially interested in how we all ended up at Bridgewater at the same time. Marlamin didn't remember anything, but she pressed Collins and Ghithgod for more information. Eventually, Collins pulled a sealed metal tube out of his pack and handed it to Caroline. He said that he found it in Bridgewater and asked her if she could read it. She said that it was incredibly precious, then very carefully unsealed it and unrolled the scroll inside. She became emotional as she read it and told us it's a prayer about the destruction of the Izuri, lamenting the loss of the people and recognizing those that remember them.

After she put the scroll away, Caroline told us that the stories on the Kithenir include the Heavens Gate, but never mention any other gates. When she heard Hazelmere talking about multiple gates in the Arcaneum, she began to wonder if there might be more than one. She said that after she left the Arcaneum, she went to Wycrof to search at the [[Hollowspire]] for a gate. However, the basement had long ago collapsed so she couldn't go deeper to continue searching. Collins asked if their stories gave a location for the Heavens Gate. Caroline answered that it was in the [[Golden City|"golden city"]] far to the east, beyond the border the [[Vidanya]] maintain along the [[Durukal Range]]. She said that their legends maintain that the Kithenir came from the east "where the sun comes from" to the west "where the sun ends." She also explained that most of the Kithenir don't live here and only come here to remember together.

Caroline pulled some papers from a bookshelf and said it was records she found on a dead scholar in the [[Vichon Order]]. One of the pages was a map of burrows that she said looked remarkable similar to the kithenir here in Mystara. There were also notes about an inscription the scholar has found in the Vichon language that included the phrase "as above, so below." Caroline then put the notes away and then told us that we had been "touched", that she could smell it on us. She explained that when the weave is pulled too much, it leaves a mark, and it had marked us.

Marlamin asked what Caroline thought the Heavens Gate was meant for. She answered that she didn't think the Izuri were the victims in the cataclysm, so that meant the Heavens Gate probably wasn't created for anything good. She then pushed Marlamin to tell more about what he knew about it and how he knew so much. Marlamin didn't want to answer, so Collins asked if he, Marlamin, and Ghithgod could have some time to talk alone. Caroline and Hazelmere left the room. 

Collins asked the rest of us whether they felt like we could trust Caroline. He also says that he didn't think she wanted to use the gates she could help us find them, so he wanted to trust her. Ghithgod said he trusted Hazelmere, who had vocally supported Caroline. Marlamin reiterated his [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|commitment]] to destroy the gates. All of us agreed that Hazelmere didn't trust the Anachreon, or any of the other major nations, and that we could convince him to help us destroy the gates. 

Meanwhile, Harkus asked Hazelmere if he thought we were trustworthy and what our moral character was like. Hazelmere said that we could be ruthless to protect each other and were loyal to each other above all else, but were generally good people. She asked why Hazelmere was willing to trust here and he said that, while she had always been open about wanting information to him, she was also one of the only people that also consistently helped him in return. 

We asked Caroline and Hazelmere to come back in and Marlamin explained his experience dying and the pact he made with [[Tymoro]]. Caroline that it was likely already too late to stop the gates from opening, since that had already begun happening, and destroying the gates would be a serious challenge. Collins pulled the gate almanac and key out of his pack. Caroline got excited seeing them and began scribbling notes on a nearby piece of paper. She then said she wants us to go meet a mapmaker that she knows, who may be able to help us find more gates. We agreed and she led us out of the room and up some final steps, opening a door into an old net shop. She opened the front door of the shop to a beautiful coastal city and welcomed us to [[Mystara]].
