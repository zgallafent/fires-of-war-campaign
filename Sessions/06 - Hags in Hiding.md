From the [[05 - Ethically Acquired Antiquities|caravan ambush site]], we walked south in the river. Once we had gone as far as we could stand the freezing water, we left the river and cut west through fields on the south side of [[Wycrof]]. Along the way [[Reb Tevye]] was spotted by a farm, but passed his presence off as some early morning fishing. Once we had circled around to the west side of Wycrof, Reb Tevye heard a bird whistling a tune he knew from his childhood. We followed the bird and it led us to a copse of trees. A stag stepped out from among the trees, then morphed into a man who introduced himself as [[Vieson]]. He told us that [[Brother Ghilain]] was waiting at the [[Stone Choir]] and he'd do his best to keep the way clear for us.

We got onto the [[Old West Road]] to continue on to the Stone Choir, but we're stopped by a patrol of soldiers on horseback. Captain Kilven, the one in charge, addressed us and demanded to know where we were going. [[Hazelmere]] answered the we were headed to a dig site in the [[Vichon Order]] and presented his [[Arcaneum]] scrip. The captain believed us, but marked the scrip so they could keep track of our story in the future. He then told us to stay off the road today. 

Finally, we reached the Stone Choir. We told Brother Ghilain about what happened and agreed to work with him to break the wards on the chests after we rested. Brother Ghilain told us that we would need to take the [[Reliquary of Vestora]] to [[Ashara]] in the [[Cindervale|Cindervale Mountains]]. When [[Collins de Jarrag Iolaire|Collins]]asked if she could come here, he said they don't have any way to contact her.

After a brief rest, Brother Ghilain, Hazelmere, and Reb Tevye spent the night breaking the wards. Inside there were a variety of ancient relics that showed the pristine quality of magical items. Brother Ghilain and Hazelmere then had a heated argument about whether these relics would go; Hazelmere wanted to take them all to Ashara and along the way make sure they weren't generous, while Brother Ghilain wouldn't let them out of his sight. The rest of us wanted to focus on resurrecting [[Marlamin]] and didn't want to escalate a conflict with Brother Ghilain, so we eventually left with only the reliquary.

After daybreak we headed north into the mountains. As we traveled, some of us heard bare feet running among the trees and a child's laughter, but we didn't see anyone. Soon after, we came across an old stone watchtower. There were two elderly women, one working the garden and the other tending the hearth we could see through the open doorway. The woman in the garden introduces herself as Ethwin, and her sister Hildred. When asked, she said they didn't know Brother Ghilain or Ashara. They invited us to stay the night, which everyone except Reb Tevye was happy to agree to.

They shared some food with us, but only [[Hank]] and Hazelmere ate any. Reb Tevye went into the privy to start casting detect magic in secret. We began to set up camp on the edge of the clearing, where Collins pulled Hazelmere aside and they agreed that they didn't want to give Brother Ghilain back the reliquary. Reb Tevye came back out of the privy and was able to detect magic on both of the women. Abruptly, Hank and Hazelmere both fell asleep, at which point Ethwin and Hildred revealed themselves to be hags and attacks us. Ghithgod and Collins woke up Hank and Hazelmere. The hags were able to severely injure Ghithgod thanks to their ability to disappear and reappear elsewhere, but we were able to kill one of them. The other vanished with an echoing laugh and didn't reappear.
