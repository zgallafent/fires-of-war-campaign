After our harrowing escape from [[Bridgewater]], we found ourselves in another cathedral-esque [[Izuri Gate System|gate room]]. There was vibration that filled the room and shook our bones. The room was completely silent and pristinely clean, as though held in stasis. We rested and slept in the room, though it was difficult to sleep.

We each had vivid dreams as we sleep. Hazelmere dreamed of the [[Daeclan Family|Daeclans]] helping him and his mother escape the attack on [[Wicksbend]] in the hidden compartment of a carriage. Then, [[Ronald Daeclan]] helping him after some students knocked papers out of his hands at the academy. Then, [[Aetheleron]] accepting him and Ronald as assistants. Then, Ronald helping him climb a steep hill on an expedition.

Then, Ronald's death. Near these gate rooms our dreams seemed to be influenced by something that makes them dark and emotionally resonant dreams; alternate darker versions of memories. This wasn't the first time we've had dreams like this since descending into Bridgewater.

After we woke up, we spent some time inspecting the gate and room. We'd hoped to find a way to get the gate working again in case we needed a way to get out of here. However, after a half hour of experiment with the key and instruction manual, we couldn't even get the key to fit into the dial.

Without having made much progress, we agreed to explore past the door and try and find out where we were. We spent some time picking through the next room, which was a puzzle room similar to Bridgewater and filled with skeletons. While searching, [[Collins de Jarrag Iolaire|Collins]] and [[Marlamin]] found a strange book that left them unsettled. [[Ghithgod]] also picked up a gleaming axe from among the bodies.

We then moved to the next room, which appeared to be the entry hall. Marlamin and Ghithgod noticed it had a rotten chemical stench. The staircase at the far end of the room has collapsed, so we began searching the rooms on either side of the gas. We found more remnants of people that had previously lived here and a possible way out through the water reservoir room. Before we could come back together to discuss our options, [[Hank]] walked across a strange spongy substance. All at once, the book and axe  the others had picked up came to life as mimics and a gibbering mouther starts riding from the ground under Hank. We managed to fight them all off, but once of the mimics managed to kill Marlamin.
