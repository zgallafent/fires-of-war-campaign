The [[Nattevagten|Nattevagten's]] began with a [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|blast of fire]] setting the roof alright, followed by someone kicking in the front and back doors. Two of them rushed in through each door, with three more a short distance away out front. One of the three out front, a dark and dour man, looked to be directing them. It was a close flight where several of us nearly died, but together with [[Ashara]] we managed to protect the [[Kivipuro|Kivipuros]] and kill all of the members of the Nattevagten.

We put out the fire, but the house was so badly damaged it would need to be fully rebuilt. We also find some money, supplies, and a black notebook of information about the Nattevagten cell and their targets.

Ashara thanked us for our help fighting them off and said she would leave with the Kivipuros, since it wasn't safe here anymore. She told Hazelmere that [[Ghilain|Brother Ghilain]] has a good heart and wants to help people. She also warned us that the Nattevagten can use arcane magic to track people and have is an amulet to block it. She said there were people in [[Wycrof]] that were trying to protect the people there and would want to be our friends. Finally, she told us that she had sense old magic on us and that strange beings were bleeding into the land. She and the Kivipuros then disappeared into the woods.
