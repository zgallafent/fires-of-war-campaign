We were all pretty worn down after [[02 - Death Beyond the Gate|fighting off]] the mimics and gibbering mouther, so we took a bit of time to rest and patch up our wounds. After that, we all started working separately. [[Collins de Jarrag Iolaire|Collins]] wanted to bring [[Marlamin|Marlamin's]] body back home to bury, so he bound it up and got it ready to carry away. We still needed to find a way out of the [[Wycrof Gate|gate complex]], so [[Ghithgod]] started climbing the tunnel above the water reservoir to see if it turned out anywhere. [[Hank]] searched other parts of the complex to see if there was anything notable we missed and Hazelmere went back to studying the [[Izuri Gate System|gate]].

After a while, Hazelmere had only understood just enough to know we'd need weeks to even start making progress on understanding how the gate works, so he rejoined the group. We all agreed we just need to get out and come back later. Ghithgod had gotten far enough up to know that the tunnel reaches the surface somewhere, but we didn't have enough rope to span the full length. We all tied on to the rope and climb together up to the surface.

When we made it to the surface we found a cave with a shrine in it. A man with an amulet to the Dawnfather was inside, praying to the shrine. Outside the cave entrance down the hill was a horse cart. He told is we were outside [[Wycrof]] and that his name is [[Reb Tevye]].

When we left the cave we found a large stone tower on a nearby hill overlooking the valley and, down the hill from the cave, a fair sized town surrounded by orchards, vineyards, and lavender fields. Tevye took us along the outskirts of Wycrof to an old decrepit temple known as the Stone Choir to introduce us to [[Ghilain|Brother Ghilain]]. He believed that Brother Ghilain could help us with Marlamin's body.

Brother Ghilain had a supernatural knowledge of us and seemed to know instinctively when we lie. He invited us in to eat and talked to us of the dangers of the Anachreon, the lost celestial artifacts he used to keep, and what we would do to bring back Marlamin. He said he will trust us with his secrets if we were willing to face the consequences, then led us into the Stone Choir. He told us we will prove worthy of his trust here, then slammed his staff into the ground. The braziers and torches erupted into flame. From the corners of the temple, shadowy figures stepped out from behind columns. Brother Ghilain said "You will prove yourself." Then the shadows attacked.
