[[Brother Ghilain]] slammed his staff into the floor of the [[Stone Choir]], channeling some unknown spell. As he did so, five masked men in [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] garb stepped out from the shadows, weapons drawn. Brother Ghilain called out that we must defeat all of them, sparing nobody. We responded in force and it turned into a bloody, close battle. At one point, [[Ghithgod]] turned his weapon on Brother Ghilain, hoping to break whatever spell he was channeling, but a blast of rediant energy threw him back. As the first few of our standard fell, the others began to flee, but [[Hank]] and [[Collins de Jarrag Iolaire|Collins]] cut them down before they could escape. As Collins fell upon the last one, their hood fell back and revealed Lef's face.

As Collins killed the last one, they all vanished in a burst of smoke. Brother Ghilain said to us, "It is done. My apologies." He healed all of our injuries.

Brother Ghilain told us about [[Velmon Arguillan]], the local magistrate of [[Wycrof]]. Velmon had taken control of the cathedral-citadel at the center of the city to study ancient relics of the gods that he had collected nearby. According to Brother Ghilain, one of these relics could resurrect [[Marlamin]] in the hands of a cleric, but the Anachreon were transporting them all to the [[Arcaneum]] in two days. 

When we asked how he knows all of this, Brother Ghilain described himself as "one who knows things." He said he was part of a group that was friends to Wycrof and to the [[Cindervale]], dedicated to "freeing the people of this land." After that he asked us to think on what he had said and rest for the night. He returned to his cabin, while we made camp in the outskirts of the woods around the Stone Choir to discuss our thoughts in private. We agreed that, while we didn't trust Brother Ghilain, we needed to go along with whatever scheme he had if we wanted a chance to resurrect Marlamin.

That night was the first restful night we had since first entering [[Bridgewater]]. In the morning, we met with Brother Ghilain for breakfast and told him we would work with him. He explained that he was part of a group known as the [[Ember Veil]], what he described as a group of patriots throughout the Anachreon. While they recognized how the Anachreon had in many ways improved the country and raised the standard of living for people within it, they believed the Anachreon had changed for the worse in the last few years. People now feared being sent to fight in pointless wars or having their children carried off to the Arcaneum; the Ember Veil wanted to stop this.

Brother Ghilain told us that the artifact we were looking for from Velmon is the [[Reliquary of Vestora]], a censer originally dedicated to the [[Raven Queen]] but later taken and co-opted by servants of the [[Pelor|Dawnfather]]. He told us that, once we recovered it, that a friend of his named [[Ashara]] could use the censer to bring our friend back.

After his explanation, he offered us some money to help us buy clothes to blend in and prepare for ambushing the caravan of artifacts when they left in a few days. He said we should pretend to be a [[Iron Consortium|Consortium]] raiding party and not leave anyone above to say otherwise. He then sent [[Reb Tevye]] off to buy clothes for us, while [[Hazelmere]] went out alone to do some errands.
