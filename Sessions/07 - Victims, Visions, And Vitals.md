After the [[06 - Hags in Hiding|surviving hag disappeared]], we went into the tower. We found a druidic laboratory, full of caged and dissected animals, natural ingredients, and scrawled research notes. The hags were apparently researching the local flora and fauna, as if they had never seen it before. The next floor were their living quarters, where they kept a variety of different disguises and a pair of boots of elvenkind in a closet. We found a trapdoor going to an attic, where we could hear something moving. The desk had maps of the [[Cindervale]] and [[Wycrof]]. [[Reb Tevye]] told us he had heard rumors of creatures from other worlds appearing near Wycrof, though none of us had heard of or seen hags before. Based on the maps, they seemed to have come from somewhere between Wycrof and the Cindervale mountains.

When we climbed up to the attic, we found a utility room with a large cistern of blood and sigils draw in blood on the floor. The body of a child was chained to a table, which had beendrained of blood and had organs removed. They had been dead for a few days. A young gnome girl cowered in one of the two cages. We broke open her cage, but she didn't respond to us until [[Hazelmere]] spoke to her in Gnomish. She told us that her name was [[Kivipuro Family|Lumi]] and she had been traveling from Wycrof with her family, [[Kivipuro Family|Arni, Helmi, and Topi]], to see [[Ashara]] when she was abducted.

Hazelmere took the hags' various notes, [[Hank]] collected and identified the boots of elvenkind, and Reb Tevye found a potion of healing and shadow dust with his detect magic spell. We then moved everything else we could from the tower outside and burned it. After that, we made camp for the night on the edge of the clearing.

The next morning, we continued north. After a few more hours of travel we reached Ashara's small cabin, where we reunited Lumi with her family. Ashara welcomed us into her home and we gave her the [[Reliquary of Vestora]]. We also warned her about the city being on high alert from when we [[05 - Ethically Acquired Antiquities|ambushed the relic caravan]]. She promised to hold the ceremony to resurrect [[Marlamin]] after we ate. During the meal, the Kivipuros told us about how they had left Wycrof because of how much the non-human persecution had grown. Ashara was helping them flee the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]]. Ashara explained that she helped protect people from the [[Nattevagten]], a secret Anachreon police force that operated in secret and was seen as a kind of bogeyman.

After we finished eating, Ashara led us in the ritual to resurrect Marlamin. She told us that we would need to sacrifice and use our willpower to bring him back through the veil of death, though it would ultimately be his choice. During the ritual, we went into a trance that allowed us to spiritually connect with him and persuade him to come back. Marlamin had been living in a pastoral paradise with his most family, but as we connected with him and began to pull on him to come back, he was visited by [[Tymoro]]. She told Marlamin that she would help him return and had chosen him to be her champion in stopping the [[Izuri Gate System|gates]] from being reopened. She told Marlamin the [[Izure|Izuri]] were going against the weave itself to try and connect the planes using the gates. She then showed us all in vision the end of the [[Fall of the Izure]]. We then were barely able to gather enough strength to pull Marlamin back and bring him to life. As we were celebrating, Ashara suddenly exclaimed that the Nattevagten had arrived, just before something lit the roof on fire and the front door blasted apart.
