The Reliquary of Vestora is an ancient religious censer.

## History
The Reliquary of Vestora was originally created as a relic for worshipping the [[Raven Queen]]. However, it was long ago stolen and co-opted by servants of the [[Pelor|Dawnfather]].

It has traditionally been held by religious figures in the [[Cindervale]]. However, it was recently taken by [[Velmon Arguillan]] for study. In 463 [[Nova Aetas|NA]], it was stolen from the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] while being transported to the [[Arcaneum]]. The thieves gave the reliquary to [[Ashara]], who has gone into hiding with it.
