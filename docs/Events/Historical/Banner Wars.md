The Banner Wars were a series of wars between the 20-30 various city states and local kingdoms within what is now the territory of the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]]. Throughout the wars, the Anachreon brought its neighbors (including [[Wycrof]]) under one banner through influence or force. The wars ended a few generations ago with the inception of the modern Anachreon empire. The only remaining human kingdom that wasn't subjugated during these wars is the [[Vichon Order]].
