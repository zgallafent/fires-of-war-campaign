The Canal Works was a project in the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] 95 years ago that involved constructing canals to connect rivers. This enabled year-round trade in the Anachreon and brought the [[Concordant Fraternity of Riverwardens]] to prominence. The canals allows the Anachreon to quickly and efficiently transport goods across the empire and avoid poor road conditions in certain parts of the year. It is also a culturally significant part of the Anachreon, representing their unity as a nation.
