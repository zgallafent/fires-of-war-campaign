The Fires of War

The droning blare of horns has been heard through out the land. War is upon the whole world. The bucolic and beautifully simple life has been confronted by the threat of fire and violence, invasion and destruction. For reasons that remain unknown to most people, three great powers of the world, or the world that is known, have risen up against each other and have clashed in great and thunderous conflict. Can you navigate this world at war and save what is most important to you?
