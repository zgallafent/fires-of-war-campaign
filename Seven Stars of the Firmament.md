The Seven Stars of the Firmament is an ancient poem. It was engraved on a pillar in the [[Izuri Gate System|gate]] room at [[Bridgewater]].

> The Seven Stars of the Firmament
> A Canticle of the Elder Vaults
> "When stone was young and silence vast
> The Voice of Flame to Heaven cast
> Seven lights to mark the sky—
> In rune and fire, they still lie high."

> ✦ Vaelun, the First Light
> "Vaelun rose, the Morning’s Cry,
> A whisper caught in dawn’s first sigh.
> From silver thought, the light was drawn,
> A spark that burned and summoned dawn."
> The first star, Vaelun, is the Rune of Beginnings, shaped of memory and wind. It marks the east.

> Ꙏ Molrûn, the Deep Ember
> "Molrûn stirred in sunless deep,
> A sullen coal where dreams still sleep.
> From stone and sorrow, night he claimed,
> And forged the dark from which stars came."
> Second was Molrûn, Forged in the Hollow Earth, a rune of weight and patience. Keeper of caverns and lost time.

> Ϟ Zariel, the Flame That Danced
> "Zariel sprang in sudden fire,
> A serpent’s leap, a tempest’s ire.
> Her light was song and fierce delight—
> The star that laughs at endless night."
> Zariel, thirdborn, is the Rune of Fire in Motion, the laughing flame that leapt across the heavens.

> Δ Thauron, the Gentle Shaper
> "Thauron bent the sky with care,
> And wove through stars the dream of air.
> A lantern’s hush, a breath so wide—
> The veil that holds the worlds inside."
> Fourth to rise, Thauron, is the Breath and Balance, shaping what came after.

> ⊕ Kelmath, the Root Star
> "Kelmath rose, the Anchor’s might,
> And drew the others into flight.
> The wheel, the stone, the still, the wise—
> A rune that binds the circling skies."
> Fifth was Kelmath, the Center, grounding the stars into a great orbit.

> ⛽ Ordulin, the Burning Path
> "Then Ordulin, wild and fierce,
> Tore burning trails the veil to pierce.
> Comet-bound and loud with flame,
> The stars still fear to speak his name."
> The sixth, Ordulin, the Roaring One, traces destruction and revelation. A burning star of omen.

> ₪ Elyndor, the Silent Crown
> "And last came Elyndor, most fair,
> Crown of cold and silver air.
> No voice it spoke, no war it made—
> It simply watched, and stars obeyed."
> Seventh and last, Elyndor the Silent, the watcher, the outermost star, calm and cold.
