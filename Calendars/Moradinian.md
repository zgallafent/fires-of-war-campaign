The Moradinian calendar is the calendar system of the [[Iron Consortium]], which is kept by the clerics and devout of [[Moradin]]. The new Moradinian Calendar is the Era of Remembrance, which was adopted in 1844 ER. The current year is 1876 ER.

# Description
As with all calendar systems, a year in the Moradinian calendar is 400 days long. Each year is divided into seven Great Seasons, or months, of 56 days each, with eight additional Holy Days at New Year. In addition to months, the calendar has a system of 7-day weeks, which makes each month exactly eight weeks long.
# History
>_When memory failed, the gods grew distant. When memory was restored, the forge was lit anew._

The Iron Consortium have had several calendars over the ages which have competed for dominance at one time or another while the Straags were independent and separate. However, in times of unity, the dwarves jointly adopt the Moradinian system.

The current iteration of the Moradinian calendar comes after a long age of distance from the gods in general. It was named the Era of Remembrance, or the "Rekindling", as it marks a re-emergence of devotion to the gods and to Moradin especially. This calendar was re-adopted after centuries of conflict and peace and unity and dissolution, when the Consortium was formed in 1844 ER.
