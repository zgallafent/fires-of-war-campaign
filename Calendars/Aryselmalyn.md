The Aryselmalyn calendar is the calendar system used by the [[Vidanya]]. It is descended from a second age of mortals after the gods removed themselves from the world. It is the oldest known system in use. It is derived from an older unknown calendar. The current year in the Aryselmalyn system is 7943 AC.

# Description
As with all systems, a year in the Aryselmalyn calendar is 400 days long. Each year is divided into 12 months of 33 days each, with four additional Sacred Days. In addition to months, the calendar has a system of 12-day weeks. Because the systems of weeks and months do not line up, each date has a monthly and weekly significance.

| Sacred Day       | Time of year              |
| ---------------- | ------------------------- |
| Vernal Awakening | First day of Spring       |
| Summer Zenith    | Longest day of the year   |
| Autumn Falling   | First day of Autumn       |
| Winter Silence   | Deepest night of the year |
