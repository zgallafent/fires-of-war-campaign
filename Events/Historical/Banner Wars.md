The Banner Wars were a series of wars between the 20-30 various city states and local kingdoms within what is now the territory of the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]]. They began when [[Eadric Vearon van Merrowe]], the new leader of the [[Arcaneum]], began aggressively expanding the Anachreon's influence throughout the [[Creon Coast]] in 1 [[Nova Aetas|NA]]. Throughout the wars, the Anachreon brought its neighbors under one banner through influence or force. The wars ended a few generations ago, at which point the only remaining human kingdom that hadn't been brought under Anachreon rule was the [[Vichon Order]]. van Merrowe's conquest and the subsequent Banner Wars are considered the to be the birth of the Anachreon empire.
