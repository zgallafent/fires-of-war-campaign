The Fall of the Izure was a conflict initiated by the unknown independent within [[Anchara]] and the gods against the [[Izure|Izure empire]] in response to the development of the [[Izuri Gate System]]. The war ended when the gods unleashed a cataclysmic force on the Izuri cities. This resulted in the complete destruction of the empire and the gates.

The main record of the war is the [[Last Saga of The Izuri]]. Additionally, when resurrecting [[Marlamin]] we all had a [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|vision]] where we saw armies surrounding an Izuri city in siege and the gods destroying the city from the heavens.
