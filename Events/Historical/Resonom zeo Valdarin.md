The Resonom zeo Valdarin (Gnomish for "The Revolution of Identity") was a war between the sea gnomes of [[Zemvalda]] and the rock gnomes of [[Losor (nation)|Losor]]. Zemvalda initiated the war against the Losor to destroy the [[Izure|Izuri]] traditions that the Losor were dedicated to preserving. It ended when Zemvalda conquered the capital of the Losor, [[Leovolan]], and incorporated the Losor into the country
