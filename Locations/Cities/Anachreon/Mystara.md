Mystara is a city in the [[Thalassian Coast]], north of the [[Vichon Order]] and part of the westernmost territories of the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]]. Its location both in a cove and at the terminus of the Old West Road makes it a major trade center in the region.

Mystara has existed at least since the end of the [[Izure|Izure empire]]. The [[Kithenir]], descendents of the Izure, arrived in Mystara fleeing the empire's collapse and found the city already populated. The records of the Kithenir mark are the earliest known records of the city.

The main port in the city is the Zaiharri pier. Most of the city is built to against the Itsahauste ("sea break" wharf), a tall stone wall holding a road along the water that small ships can dock at.
