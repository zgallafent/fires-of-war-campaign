Leovolan is the largest city in [[Zemvalda]]. It is located in the mountains on the eastern edge of the country near the [[Vidanya|Vidanyan]] border. It is the homeland of the rock gnomes and former capital of the [[Losor (nation)|Losor]].

When gnomes first settled the current-day territory of Zemvalda, the gnomes that settled Losor primarily survived on terrace farming. This allowed their population to grow much faster than their cousins in [[Tirnavis]]. However, poor land management has degraded the land over time, causing Leovolan to shift its economy towards mining and production. The mountains around the city are rich in a variety of minerals, which are processed in the city and then shipped out through Tirnavis to trade for foodstuff.

Despite the wealth flowing through the city, the trade is generally controlled by sea gnome overseers. This means that the rock gnomes population is generally poorer in Leovolan than the rest of the country. Despite the reverence that these gnomes have for the city as their racial home, many have been migrating towards the coast or out of Zemvalda to search for better opportunities.
