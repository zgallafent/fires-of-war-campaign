Dornstahl is an ancient city between the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] and [[Iron Consortium]]. The city is contested territory between the Anachreon and the Consortium with no clear controller.

## History 
At the beginning of the [[docs/Events/Historical/Banner Wars|Banner Wars]], Dornstahl was a town too small to be named on the outskirts of a human kingdom. As the wars went on, the kingdom's influence over the city fell. By 200 [[Nova Aetas|NA]], enough dwarves had moved to the town from [[Hallenstad]] and [[Dämmerhall]] that it became a part of Straag Hallenstad, where it gained the name Dornstahl.

In the end of the 4th century of the Nova Aetas, the Anachreon began reconquering the lands dwarves had settled south of [[Dornquar]] in the last few centuries. By the turn of the 5th century, the Anachreon borders had nearly reached Dornstahl. When the Iron Consortium formed in 431 NA, it formally declared its borders to include Dornstahl. The resulting war has seen heavy fighting around Dornstahl for decades, which had left the city scarred and with no clear ruler.
