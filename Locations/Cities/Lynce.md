Lynce is an ancient city between the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] and [[Iron Consortium]]. The city is culturally most similar to the [[Vichon Order]], but it's contested territory between the Anachreon and the Consortium with no clear controller.

## History 
At the beginning of the [[docs/Events/Historical/Banner Wars|Banner Wars]], Lynce was a minor city on the outskirts of a human kingdom. As the wars went on, the kingdom's influence over the city fell. By 200 [[Nova Aetas|NA]], enough dwarves had moved to the city from [[Hallenstad]] and [[Dämmerhall]] that it became a part of Straag Hallenstad.

In the end of the 4th century of the Nova Aetas, the Anachreon began reconquering the lands dwarves had settled south of [[Dornquar]] in the last few centuries. By the turn of the 5th century, the Anachreon borders had nearly reached Lynce. When the Iron Consortium formed in 431 NA, it formally declared its borders to include Lynce. The resulting war has seen heavy fighting around Lynce for decades, which had left the city scarred and with no clear ruler.
