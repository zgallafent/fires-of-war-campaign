Wicksbend is a city on a river between the [[Anachreon (nation)]] and the [[Vidanya]], named after the old explorer Jeremy Wickens that founded it. It was originally part of the Anachreon, but had recently been conquered by the Vidanya. Because of its proximity to [[Zemvalda]], it served as the border city for the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon's]] diplomats, soldiers, and traders. It also boasted a large academy, built on ancient ruins of what many believe to be an old [[Izure|Izuri]] library. Students from the Anachreon, Zemvalda, and occasionally [[Skjoldhelm]] would come to study to Wicksbend in order to meet and share ideas with students from the other countries, before returning to their own academic centers.

The Vidanya targeted Wicksbend early in the war. Because the [[Old West Road]] was well guarded, Wicksbend served as a way for Vidanyan troops to enter the Anachreon. They also hoped to search for any possible [[Izure|Izuri]] artifacts from the original library, or that has been brought for study.

When the Vidanya took the city, they immediately shut down the academy for "unnatural and profane studies." Many of the leading academics were killed and any students from Zemvalda or Skjoldhelm were sent back to their homes. The city has since been under martial rule as the Vidanya cleanses the academy.
