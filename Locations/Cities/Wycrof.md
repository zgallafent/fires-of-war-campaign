Wycrof is a city on the western side of the [[Anachreon (nation)]]. It's a lush breadbasket full of orchards and vineyards. Since the war has started, the town has been filled by soldiers and propaganda posters.

A city council ostensibly directs the city, but magistrate [[Velmon Arguillan]] is the only one that holds actual power.
