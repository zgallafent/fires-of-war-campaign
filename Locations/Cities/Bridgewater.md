Bridgewater, or Zarrak as it's known by the dwarves and Nenalant by the elves, is an Izuri fortress in [[Anachreon (nation)]] territory. A few months ago researchers from the Anachreon moved in to research an Izuri artifact, possibly known as the [[Heavens Gate]]

In the depths of the keep is a cathedral-like room containing an [[Izuri Gate System|Izuri gate]]. It's an octagonal room with large seven runes carved into the floor. The walls, pillars, and dome of the room are all filled with engravings. The bridge is on a raised dais in the center of the room with a dial in front of it.

The following engraving are each on a pillar:
"The sum of the three eldest stars guides the hand."
The entirety of [[Seven Stars of the Firmament]], along with numeric values by each star (2, 5, 6, 1, 4, 7, 9 in the order of the poem).

Some of the symbols along the walls match the [[Gate Almanac|gate almanac]], as well as four that represent planets in the solar system: [[Varkum]], [[Sylva]], [[Dromos]], and [[Elarin]]. Beneath the engraved symbols for the planets it says "The time it takes them to dance across the floor is the square of their distance."

The dome of the ceiling is engraved around the sides with constellations.
Along the lip of the dome is engraved:
"When the twin serpents pass beneath the hunter's bow, the seal may open."
It's all engraved in old elvish.
