The Laurel Crown Inn is an inn owned by [[Agathe Brelin]] in [[Wycrof]]. It is also a base for local [[Ember Veil]] operations in the [[Cindervale]].

The Inn has a dining area on the first floor, which is often where [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreonan]] somewher soldiers gather while off duty. The upper floor is rented rooms. There is a stable attached to the building.
