The Wycrof Gate is an [[Izuri Gate System|Izuri gate]] beneath the hills on the outskirts of [[Wycrof]]. The main entrance to the gate complex collapsed long ago and the [[03 - Ascent to Wycrof|only known entrance]] is hidden beneath a shrine to the Dawnfather (?). We discovered it during the [[01 - Escape from Bridgewater|escape from Bridgewater]]. [[Marlamin]] died while [[02 - Death Beyond the Gate|exploring]] it.

The entry hall of the gate complex is a large long room with large pillars and statues lining the room. The floor was covered in unfamiliar engravings. The start of the room, which used to be a grand stone staircase, has caved in. There are two rooms on each side of the hall. The left room nearest to the collapsed stairs is a water reservoir, which has a 300-foot vertical tunnel leading up to the shrine above. The far left room is an old loving quarters. The right room nearest to the stairs is a study. The far right room is unexplored.

On the far side of the entry hall through a door is some kind of puzzle room based on the towers of Hanoi that used to control the doors in the complex. In the center of the room is a plinth with similar carvings to the entrance hall. The walls The doors to the entry hall were already open and we opened the doors to the gate room when we arrived through the gate. Through a side door in this room is a small study And the main door on the opposite side of the room leads to the gate room.

After we first arrived, we found signs throughout the complex, excluding the gate room, that many people have lived and died here. Many rooms had signs that people had lived there for an extended period of time. Humanoid skeletons, possibly human or elven, were piled against the door to the gate room as if it was the only place the inhabitants could leave the dead.
