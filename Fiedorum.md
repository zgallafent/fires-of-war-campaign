Fiedorum is a unique material only ever found in the composition of [[Izure|Izuri]] gates. It is named after [[Fiedorek]], where it was initially discovered. It's incredibly durable and lightweight. Texturally it bears similarities to ceramic, being very lightweight and durable, but also has many metallic properties and conducts energy better than any other metal. There is no known method to create it. It also has no known melting point, as attempts to do so have only succeeded in making it glow red-hot with heat.
