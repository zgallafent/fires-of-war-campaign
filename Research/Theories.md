The [[Seven Stars of the Firmament]] are the seven different gates of the [[Izuri Gate System]].
- Vaelun is the furthest east gate
- Molrûn or Zariel is [[Fiedorek]]
- Kelmath is [[Bridgewater]] and the center of the gate system

In Bridgewater it says, "The time it takes them to dance across the floor is the square of their distance." Could this mean the number on the dial needs to be the sum of the squares of the distances of the source and destination runes? The rune used on the dial to go to the unknown gate was [[Sylva]] (2) and the number used was 13, so it would add up with [[Dromos]] (3). Alternatively, it used the serpent rune, which could match Zariel from the Seven Stars, which was third in the poem.
