Ronald Daeclan was a young member of the [[Daeclan Family]]. I became his friend during our adolescent-level studies and we stayed together through our studies at the academy in [[Wicksbend]]. When the Vidanya took the city we eventually were able to escape to the [[Arcaneum]]. There he specialized in magotech and artifice; I learned most of what I know on the subject from watching him. After I began working with [[Aetheleron]] on researching the [[Izuri Gate System]], I encouraged him to join us and he officially apprenticed under Aetheleron.

During the [[01 - Escape from Bridgewater|escape from Bridgewater]], Aetheleron went mad and killed Ronald with a fireball. I still haven't had a true chance to mourn his death. I have to grab his family band from his body in [[Bridgewater]] so I can eventually return it to his family.
