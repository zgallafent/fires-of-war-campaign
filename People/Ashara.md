Ashara is a priest and a member of the [[Ember Veil]]. She helped the Ember Veil by guiding people that were being hunted by the [[Nattevagten]] into hiding, operating out of her hidden home in the [[Cindervale|Cindervale Mountains]].

In 463 [[Nova Aetas|NA]], the [[Kivipuro|Kivipuro family]] arrived at her home looking to leave [[Wycrof]]. The next day, a band of adventures [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|brought her]] the [[05 - Ethically Acquired Antiquities|stolen]] [[Reliquary of Vestora]], which she used to help them resurrect their fallen friend [[Marlamin]]. Nattevagten operatives hunting the Kivipuros followed one of these two groups and discovered her home, so she has since gone into hiding along with the Reliquary.

## Player Relationship
### Affection
**4/6**
I only knew Ashara for all of one evening before she went into hiding, so I can't say I know her well. However, she seems like a very amicable person.
### Respect
**6/6**
Resurrection magic isn't just difficult, it's practically unheard of. I've heard rumors of it happening among mages at the Arcaneum, but never anything that's been confirmed. Even with the help of the Reliquary of Vestora, she has to be incredibly powerful to accomplish that 
### Trust
**5/6**
Ashara has already helped us a year deal and has a proven record of fighting to protect the people suffering most from the war. As long as we're working to stop the war, I expect she'd be willing to help us if we only had a way to contact her.
