Narnod is my cousin in [[Zemvalda]]. When we were children he came to live with us in [[Wicksbend]] to study. After we finished our schooling, he moved to Zemvalda to work while I continued with academy preparations and research. We stayed in touch through letters until my home was destroyed. Since then we've been unable to keep up contact as I've traveled around the Anachreon. He had repeatedly invited me to return to Zemvalda, where my extended family would welcome us in, but I always felt my research was too important to abandon.
