Jorren was a young man that lived in [[Wycrof]]. He worked as a stablehand at the [[Laurel Crown Inn]] and helped provide the [[Ember Veil]] with supplies.

He helped a band of adventures [[05 - Ethically Acquired Antiquities|steal]] a collection of artifacts from the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] by laying a false trail to lure away the guards investigating the attack. However, he was caught and [[09 - Anachreon Avenged|executed]] by [[Velmon Arguillan]].
