Brother Ghilain is an old human man that  lives [[Wycrof]]. He is blind monk now, but looks to have once been a strong and powerful warrior. He has a long scar running across his eyes, seemingly caused by a mix of blade and burn. He believes the Anachreon has become too ambitious and will break the way it is going. He says they defile the holy.
## Early Life
Ghilain became an acolyte of the [[Pelor|Dawnfather]] when he was young. He fought in the [[docs/Events/Historical/Banner Wars|Banner Wars]] for Wycrof when it was its own nation, possibly for the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] as well after it incorporated Wycrof.
## Priest in Wycrof
Known as Brother Ghilain in Wycrof, he is the caretaker of the [[Stone Choir]]. People in the city generally view him as a decrepit old man. However, he is actively working with the [[Ember Veil]] to sabotage the Anachreon in the region. Ghilain believes that the Anachreon's ambition is driving it to ruin and that it defiles the holy. His proclaimed goal is to liberate the people of the [[Cindervale]].

Ghilain describes his role as "one who knows things." He has connections with [[Agathe Brelin]], [[Ashara]], [[Reb Tevye]], and [[Vieson]].

In 463 [[Nova Aetas|NA]], he coordinated with a group of adventures to steal a collection of the religious artifacts, which he claims were originally taken from their rightful owners in Wycrof when it was conquered by the Anachreon. The adventurers intercepted the caravan of artifacts when they were being sent by [[Velmon Arguillan]] to the [[Arcaneum]] and delivered them to Ghilain.

Ghilain and I had a pretty intense argument about who can be trusted with the artifacts we recovered from the Anachreon. We somewhat made amends when I came back from finding Ashara, but there's clearly still a lot of tension between us.
### Respect
**5/6**
When we first met Ghilain, he summoned shadowy figures to attack us as a test. That requires magic certainly beyond what I'm capable of.

Ghilain has hidden several powerful religious artifacts hidden away somewhere. We have no idea what any of them do, but he sent us with the [[Reliquary of Vestora]] to Ashara, and that at last has the power to resurrect.
### Trust
**4/6**
Ghilain openly invited us to stay and work with him and the Ember Veil. However, he's always made sure to give us only the smallest scraps of information we need to complete his tasks and any help he gives comes with a price. At least he seems to be on our side for now.

He hates the Anachreon, which I still consider to be my home, so I worry that may bring us into conflict later.

Ashara has vouched for his character and that he only wants to help people. While I don't know her very well, her endorsement makes me want to try and trust him.
