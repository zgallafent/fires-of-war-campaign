Ellen Moore is a fellow academic I met while briefly staying in the [[Arcaneum]] to work with [[Erik Skjoldson]]. We were introduced on our shared interest in the gates system developed by the [[Izure]]. From the moment we met our relationship has been poor and in the years since it has developed into an academic rivalry. Her theory is that the gate system was a novel invention created wholesale from the arcane magics practiced by the Izure, while I have proposed that it comes from a system that outdates even them, a more primal form of travel. The last time I saw her was just after I had met to discuss my theories on a connection between the [[Heavens Gate]] and the Vidanyan divine realm. I don't know for sure, but I suspect she may have overheard at least some of that conversation.
