Velmon Arguillan is the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] magistrate in [[Wycrof]]. While the city council still exists, he abrogated their power and so that he has complete authority over the city.

Velmon's main focus in Wycrof is collections ancient relics from the [[Cindervale]], researching them, and then sending them to the [[Arcaneum]]. When the most recent shipment was [[05 - Ethically Acquired Antiquities|intercepted]], he personally [[09 - Anachreon Avenged|executed]] [[Jorren]] as a traitor.
