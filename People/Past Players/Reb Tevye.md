Reb Tevye, formerly Dan's character, is a human cleric in [[Wycrof]]. He and his wife used to be healers for the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] army, but he left after she died in a battle. He took up religious servitude as [[Brother Ghilain]]'s acolyte at the [[Stone Choir]]. He also occasionally helped members of the [[Ember Veil]] as a porter.

We met Reb Tevye after [[03 - Ascent to Wycrof|finding our way out]] of the [[Wycrof Gate|Wycrof gate complex]]. He saw that we had [[Marlamin]]'s body and told us that Father Ghilain would be able to help. He helped us as we [[05 - Ethically Acquired Antiquities|intercepted]] a caravan of religious artifacts on the way to the [[Arcaneum]] and and as we took the [[Reliquary of Vestora]] to [[Ashara]] to resurrect Marlamin. Once we finished and returned to Wycrof, he remained behind in his home in the Cut to continue working with Brother Ghilain and the Ember Veil.
