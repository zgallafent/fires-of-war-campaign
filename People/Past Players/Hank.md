Hank was Ron's character, a human ranger from the [[Iron Consortium]]. He claims to have been adopted by gnomes in [[Zemvalda]] when his family passed away, but [[Brother Ghilain]] said he was lying.

After we [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|resurrected]] [[Marlamin]], we parted ways with Hank in [[Wycrof]]. He told us he was headed to [[Basilique]] to find passage home on a merchant ship.
