Collins de Jarrag Iolaire, Kyle's character, is a human warrior from the [[Vidanya]]. After being banished from his home, Collins has been traveling across [[Anchara]]. During that time he had befriended [[Ghithgod]], [[Hank]], [[01 - Escape from Bridgewater|Lef]], and [[Marlamin]], who became a second family to him. Though he isn't aligned with any particular nation or faction, he has taken part in battles on the side of the [[Iron Consortium]]. He has a strong dislike towards the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]].

Collins was a member of the Consortium raiding party, led by [[Gurlurek]], that broke through Anachreon battle lines to enter their research site at [[Bridgewater]]. When we [[01 - Escape from Bridgewater|fled]] though the [[Izuri Gate System|gate]], he was the first to confront [[Hazelmere]] over his Anachreon allegiance and the most distrustful of him. 

The combination of Lef's death in the battle at Bridgewater and [[Marlamin]]'s [[02 - Death Beyond the Gate|death]] shortly after nearly broke Collins's spirit. He wholly committed himself to finding a way to bring Marlamin back to life at any cost, which we barely [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|accomplished]].

## Player Relationship
### Affection
**4/6**
When they first met, Collins seemed very content to hold Hazelmere at arm's length, and Hazelmere wasn't going to go out of his way to change that. However, fighting for your life together tends to break down that icyness. While he may not been comfortable enough to truly be friends with Collins yet, Hazelmere it at least content working with and travelling alongside him.
### Respect
**5/6**
Hazelmere hadn't didn't much time around soldiers, but he struggled to imagine that many could go toe-to-toe with Collins. He knew his way around a blade, and while Hazelmere might not put much stock in Collins long-term planning he couldn't deny that Collins is a brilliant tactician. When they were struggling to survive the storm on N'duka's ship, Collins managed to support every one of them while also keeping the ship held together by a thread.
### Trust
**5/6**
In the moments after Marlamin's resurrection, Collins and Hazelmere locked eyes and some kind of understanding passed between them. Hazelmere had fought and bled with the rest to bring Collins's family back and that means something. While Hazelmere knew he couldn't expect the same loyalty from Collins that Marlamin had, he could always trust Collins to have his back. They'd come a long way from when Collins wouldn't even trust him with a weapon.
