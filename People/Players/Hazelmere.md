Hazelmere is a rock gnome archaeologist and scholar from the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]]. He was a core member of the [[Arcaneum]] research team studying the [[Izuri Gate System]] and is travelling with us to discover what they are and how they work. 

Hazelmere was born to Bolren and Lamara in [[Leovolan]], the ancestral home of the rock gnomes. Shortly after his birth, his parents left the city to escape the industrial lower class of the and moved with him to [[Wicksbend]].

Growing up in an Anachreon city afforded Hazelmere an opportunity to receive a childhood education at Wickebend's youth academy. During this time he befriended [[Ronald Daeclan]], a human from a minor noble family in the city.
