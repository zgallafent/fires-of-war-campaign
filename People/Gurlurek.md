Gurlurek was a dwarf from the [[Iron Consortium]], possible from the region around [[Bridgewater]]. He lead a party into Bridgewater to collapse the way to the [[Izuri Gate System|Izuri Gate]] inside and ensure nobody lived to tell of its existence. However, he was killed in the [[01 - Escape from Bridgewater|battle]] in the gate room.
