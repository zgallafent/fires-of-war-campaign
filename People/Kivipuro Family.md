The Kivipuro family is a Gnomish family from [[Wycrof]]. They fled the city from fear of the ramping persecution towards non-humansb and have gone into hiding with the help of [[Ashara]].

The family, consists of the parents, Arni and Helmi; the elder son, Topi; and the younger daughter, Lumi. Before they left Wycrof, Arni and Lumi ran a shop in town. Over several years they saw druid, cleric, and halfling communities in the [[Cindervale]] driven out. Arni attributed this effort to the [[Nattevagten]], though they had no proof. They eventually decided to leave home and were directed by members of the [[Ember Veil]] to find Ashara in the Cindervale Mountains.

While traveling to Ashara's home, Lumi disappeared from their camp during the night. We [[06 - Hags in Hiding|found]] her in an old watchtower where the hags that had abducted her were living. The Kivipuros arrived at Ashara's home not long before we did, where Lumi and her family reunited.

After the Nattevagten [[07 - Victims, Visions, And Vitals|attacked]] Ashara's home, Ashara decided to go into hiding along with them. Before they left, [[Hazelmere]] told them they could go to his cousin [[Narnod]] for help if they were ever in [[Tirnavis]].
