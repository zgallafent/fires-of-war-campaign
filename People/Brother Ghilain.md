Brother Ghilain is an old monk living at an ancient decrepit temple near Wycrof. He is blind, but looks to have once been a strong and powerful man. He has a long scar running across his eyes, seeking caused by a mix of blade and burn. He says to have known war even before the current. He follows the Dawnfather since he was young. He fought in the Anachreon unification campaigns and for Wycrof when it was its own nation. He believes the Anachreon has become too ambitious and will break the way it is going. He says they defile the holy. He claims many artifacts of the gods were in Wycrof before it was conquered by the Anachreon. Many of the artifacts were taking by the Magistrate of the Anachreon.
