The Mapmaker is a [[Vichon Order|Vichian]] cartographer. He served the last king of the Vichon Order decades ago. When the king fell, the Mapmaker carried off many of their records in secret and went into hiding. 

The Mapmaker may be able to help us find more [[Izuri Gate System|Izuri Gates]]. [[Caroline Harkus]] knows how to find him.
