The Daeclan family is a prominent human family in [[Wicksbend]]. They first rose to power a few generations ago thanks to Vernus Daeclan, an inventor mage famous by his discovery of the rebound wave reaction. He built up a generational wealth that the family uses to sponsor researchers and new academics in Wicksbend. They use discoveries and inventions from the people they patronize to expand their wealth and influence.

I became acquainted with the Daeclans though [[Ronald Daeclan]]. When I entered the academy they were my sponsor.
