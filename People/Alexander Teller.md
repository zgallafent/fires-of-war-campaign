Alexander Teller was a researcher from the [[Arcaneum]] that had been assigned to assist [[Aetheleron]] at [[Bridgewater]]. He was studying the weave and its interaction with the mundane world and had been developing a thesis on binding points between the weave and our plane. I first met him at the academy in the Anachreon Arcaneum, where we had been collaborating on his thesis. He had been excited to join our expedition to Bridgewater, hoping that it would be the puzzle piece to his thesis, but was eventually killed when we were ambushed by giant spiders in Bridgewater.

Alexander and I weren't what I would call friends, but we had spent a lot of time working together and I knew his thesis would have been a huge milestone in his field. I hope I can find the evidence he needed to prove his thesis in the [[Izuri Gate System]].
