The Kithenir, formally known as Izurthenar Velun and informally as Mystarathen or the people of [[Mystara]], is an ethnic group originating from the [[Izure]]. They traditionally maintain the history of the Izure through oral folklore and written records and continue to search for other lost records of the empire.

When the [[Fall of the Izure|empire's collapse]], refuges travelled out west and established a home in Mystara. Whether they founded the city or the city was already here is unknown, but their descendents are the Kithenir.

They live throughout the former territory of the Izure, but regularly migrate to their hidden underground home in Mystara also referred to as a kithenir. This serves both as an archive of history and a home for developing Kitherin families.

Although the Kithenir are directly descended from the Izuri, they do not identify themselves as Izuri.

The Kithenir is currently gathered to conduct their ceremonies; notably a high elder has died and a going woman has come to age.
