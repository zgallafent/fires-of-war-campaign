The Iron Consortium begun as a union of Dwarven kingdoms and guilds, but has grown to extend to other races that have been subjugated and ostracized by the Kingdoms of Men and Elves. The Iron Consortium began as a manufacturing and trade guild, but has grown in power and has swelled in numbers. It is a diverse and sometimes messy collective, but what is lacking in order is made up for in pure drive and determination. They draw strength from a capacity to produce the means and weapons of war. The Consortium have been pushed to unify and are seeking to reclaim old dwarfish lands and Kingdoms.  They believe in the freedom of the short folk to live in pride and power.  They seem to have regained their former glory.  They have very recently learned of a secret concerning Izuri artifacts and have begun a rush to beat the Anachreon and Vidanya to them.

Anachreonan propaganda describes the Consortium as brutal draconian. They can barely feed their people and  the temples and clerics decide who gets healing or whether they can get clean drinking water.

The Iron Consortium is built on the grievances of dozens of kingdoms that haven been swallowed up and countless cultures and peoples being mistreated or consumed by the other factions. As the continent was being carved up, many of the people that didn't fit into the mold of the Archaneum or the Voldanya, were given refuge in the Consortium. The dwarfs were not explicitly unified, but when the other two became a cohesive power, they joined together to protect their claims. 

Key details: Light on air support. Some Dirigibles, but weakest on air.

HEALING: Rely on home remedies and some traditional druidic healing as well as some cleric healing. Because of the fractured nature of the realm, they have a complex culture which is strongly Dwarvish, but with many other races, cultures and religious observations. It is accepted that peoples will maintain their own observances inside of some standards of society.
