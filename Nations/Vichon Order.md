The Vichon Order is a semi-autonomous nation bordered on the north by the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] and the south by the [[Iron Consortium]]. It considered itself an independent nation and people and is neutral in the current conflict. However, it's heavily influenced by the Anachreon, who would like to claim it as a part of their rule as a human nation.

The Vichon Order was once a united kingdom, ruled by a single king. However, a few decades ago the king lost power and control of the nation fractured between various lords. Although they sometimes work together to protect the Order's identity as an independent, there too much conflict between the lords for them to present a truly united front against the Anachreon or Iron Consortium.

I, as part of a research team led by [[Aetheleron]], conducted an archaeological dig at an ancient watchtower and aquaduct within the Order. We discovered an inscription that describes a key that could be used to control gates like the one discovered in [[Fiedorek]].
