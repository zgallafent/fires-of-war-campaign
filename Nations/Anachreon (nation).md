The Anachreon is the nation capitaled by the city of the [[Arcaneum]]. It was originally a small Izuri research library that survived the fall of [[Izure]] and grew out of the ashes. It eventually grew into a city-state. It consolidated knowledge, power, and influences over the last two millennium to the point where they have gone from creating the instruments of war, to now using them with their own armies. Their influence in trade, manufacturing, and research have created an enormous amount of wealth and power. This cumulated in the [[Banner Wars]], when the Anachreon brought the few dozen other nearby human kingdoms around them together under a single banner, often by force. The only remaining kingdom they haven't brought in yet is the [[Vichon Order]], which is strongly influenced by the Anachreon but considered itself an independent nation and people. The Anachreon has avoided forcing the issue in case it would lead to open conflict.

The Anachreon is ostensibly ruled by the Throne, an emperor over the whole country elected by the council of the [[Arcaneum|Arcaneum]]. However, much of the ruling power truly comes from the [[Council of Eight]], a group of representatives from the significant guilds and colleges that do a lot of the legislative work in the country. The Throne and the Council of Eight are engaged in a constant power struggle, both been each other and among the guilds, but present a unified front to the people. **I only vaguely know about the push and pull among the guilds.** Local rule outside of the Arcaneum is handled by Magistrates, which each rule over the different kingdoms that were brought under Anachreon rule.

The Anachreon is the smallest in numbers compared to the [[Iron Consortium]] and the [[Vidanya]], but outsized in their use of magic and arcane creations. They have recently begun aggressively expanding and are searching for Izuri artifacts.

Centered on the city library of Anachreon at the mouth of the Thyssalian River delta. A powerful shipping and mercantile hub. Their domain is aligned along the coast of the north and western low land and plains. They have agricultural and commerce strength and bring loyalty through prosperity.

Key Details: Air ships. Strongest by air and sea. The air ships are powered by arcane engines that are novel and not understood very much at all by the other factions.

HEALING: Has begun to develop based on scientific and arcane exploration. The mages resent the idea of having to rely on gods and clerics to heal and defend against disease. They want to have their own power to keep themselves safe and healthy. 

Uses the Anachreon crownmark as currency.

Throughout the Anachreon generally one of every 500 people have magical aptitude. Often their brought from their towns to the Arcaneum to hone their talent for the good of the Anachreon.
