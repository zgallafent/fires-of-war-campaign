The Izure (more commonly known as the Old Ones, Ancient Ones, or Ancients) was an empire that controlled all of [[Anchara]]. They spread their influence across the region using the [[Izuri Gate System]], a series of arcane gates that could transport people between them instantaneously. They were wiped out around 2,100 ago and their ruins serve as the foundations of many modern cities, such as the [[Anachreon (city)]].

They're described in fairy tales and bedtime stories as being able to travel in the blink of an eye. Generally they are the subject of horror stories or warning fables.

## The Fall of Izure
The fall of Izure was a cataclysmic event that wiped out the entire empire in a very short period of time. The [[Last Saga of the Izuri]] describes it as a "terrible storm", which shook the ground and rent the air. The storm destroyed entire cities and supposedly cursed the land for a thousand years.

### Vidanya
The Vidanya hate the Izure for bringing about a global calamity through their research into arcane magic. They believe that the fall of the Izure was foretold, or possibly even sent, by the gods.
