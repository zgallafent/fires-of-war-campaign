The Losor (derived from the word for "faithful" or "obedient") was a nation of rock gnomes within the modern territory of [[Zemvalda]]. It existed from the [[Fall of the Izure|fall of the Izuri empire]] until the [[Resonom zeo Valdarin]]. Its capital city was [[Leovolan]].

After the fall of the Izure, many rock gnomes remained loyal to the empire. In the face of Zemvaldan persecution for their loyalty, they formed the Losor to defend themselves and force Zemvalda out. This led to the Resonom zeo Valdarin, which resulted in the capture of Leovolan and end of the Losor as a nation.
