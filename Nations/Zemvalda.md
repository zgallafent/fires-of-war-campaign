Zemvalda is a Gnomish kingdom on the northern coast of [[Anchara]]. It is bordered by the [[Vidanya]] to the south and east. Zemvalda's capital city is [[Tirnavis]] and its largest city is [[Leovolan]]. The country is almost exclusively inhabited by rock and sea gnomes. The name Zemvalda is derived from the Gnomish words for "child" (*zema*) and "free" (*valda*).

The age of the Zemvalda is a contested issue. [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreonan]] archaeologists date the first records of any settlements to after the [[Izure]] incorporated the region into their empire. However, Gnomish tradition claims Zemvaldan gnomes were the first race to inhabit [[Anchara]]. According to their belief, the god [[Kord]] drives waves against the rocky coastal shores in his efforts to reach out to [[Sehanine]] and the gnomes were formed from the churning rocks and waves. From there, the gnomes spread across the modern territory of Zemvalda.

As the gnomes settled the region, they divided into two groups, which are now known as sea gnomes and rock gnomes. The sea gnomes founded Tirnavis and survived on fishing. The rock gnomes moved further inland to founded Leovolan and subsisted primarily on agriculture. These groups lack any physical distinctions that are significant enough to qualify as a full subrace under Arcaneum classification. However, they differ enough culturally that they're generally referred to as subraces.

After the [[Fall of the Izure|Izure's collapse]], the gnomes divided into two separate nations: Zemvalda and the [[Losor (nation)|Losor]]. While Zemvalda hated the Izure and wanted to remove any records of them, the Losor remained loyal. This led to a series of wars that eventually ended with the [[Resonom zeo Valdarin]], the sea gnomes took control of Leovolan and subjugated the rock gnomes.

The nation now exists in two castes, with the sea gnomes controlling the government and subjecting the rock gnomes. After the Resonom, Zemvalda conducted a propaganda to turn the rock gnomes against their Izuri heritage, which was largely successful. This has created a lasting peace between the two groups despite the power dynamic between them, though resistance still exists in a rebel movement known as the [[Losor (organization)|Losor]].

Zemvalda is most closely aligned with the Anachreon because of trade with [[Fiedorek]] the city [[Anachreon (city)|Anachreon]] through the [[Nordsae]]. However, it also maintains a relationship with the [[Iron Consortium]] and the [[Vidanya]] and it officially maintains a neutral stance in the current war.
