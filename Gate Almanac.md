The gate almanac is a collection of plates made of [[Fiedorum]], engraved with instructions on how to use the [[Izuri Gate System]]. The engravings are mostly in the [[Izure|Izuri]] language, but some sections are in languages rooted in draconic or elvish. The languages all match the [[Last Saga of The Izuri]].
