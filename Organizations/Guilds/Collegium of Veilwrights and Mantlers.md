The Collegium of Veilwrights and Mantlers is a guild of clothing, banners, symbology, and culture in the [[Anachreon (nation)]] and a member guild of the [[Council of Eight]]. Thruey rose to power in the [[Banner Wars]], by providing the Anachreon's army with uniforms and signal banners that could resist rain and alchemical fire. They tied clothing to morale and politics, eventually turning veils into a symbol of civic pride.

They're associated with the [[Chromatic Cloisters]] in the [[Arcaneum]].

Much of their political comes from manipulating symbols and culture in the Anachreon, but also predominantly control clothes trade. Their closest ally is the [[League of Salt and Silver]].
