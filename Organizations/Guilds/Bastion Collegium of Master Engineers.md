The Bastion Collegium of Master Engineers is a guild of magical engineers and stone shapers in the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] and a member guild of the [[Council of Eight]]. They rose to prominence by developing the technology to harness arcane energies that make air travel possible. They eventually merged with human engineers to gain state funding, which over time has caused the dwarves to be pushed out by the Anachreon's xenophobic tendencies.

They're associated with the [[Bastionum]] in the [[Arcaneum]].

The Engineers are the largest warmongers among the Council of Eight, since it's incredibly profitable for them. They're constantly in conflict with the [[Order of Chain and Flame]] over credit for weapons inventions.
