The Conclave of the Amberglass is a guild of glassblowers and alchemists in the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] and a member guild of the [[Council of Eight]]. Their presence slowly grew alongside the demand for glass in alchemical experiments, eventually cumulating in their invention of highly stable and arcane resistant glass. Their breakthroughs allowed for huge advancements in science and technology.

They're associated with the [[Prismatica]] in the [[Arcaneum]].

Their guild is one of the most significant to the Arcaneum because of its constant need for glass. They're also courted in secret by the [[Ember Veil]]. They have little economic power compared to the other guilds, but have immense cultural and mystical prestige.
