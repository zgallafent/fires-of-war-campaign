The Syndicate of Timber and Crown is a guild that manages land use and lumber harvests in the [[Anachreon (nation)]] and a member guild of the [[Council of Eight]]. They were originally an independent group born from halfling stewardship traditions. During the [[Great Fires]] they incorporated into the Anachreon with royal backing to enforce controlled harvests. The Syndicate once was a good mix of humans and halflings, which gave them legitimacy with the common folk. However, that mixture has shifted in favor of humans and now the few remaining halflings in leadership are under heavy pressure.

They're associated with the [[Verdant Hall]] in the [[Arcaneum]].

They don't have as much direct power as the other guilds, but because of how involved land is in every industry they have broad power throughout the Anachreon. They particularly come into conflict with the [[Tribunal of the Golden Sheaf]] over land use rights.
