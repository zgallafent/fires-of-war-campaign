The Syndicate of Timber and Crown is a guild that manages land use and lumber harvests in the [[Anachreon (nation)|Anachreon]] and a member guild of the [[Council of Eight]]. It is associated with the [[Verdant Hall]] in the [[Arcaneum]].

They don't have as much direct power as the other guilds, but because of how involved land is in every industry they have broad power throughout the Anachreon. They particularly come into conflict with the [[Tribunal of the Golden Sheaf]] over land use rights.

## History
Land stewardship in the [[Creon Coast]] rises from the traditions of the halflings that were once a major demographic in the region. Several independent groups separate formed from these traditions. During the [[Events/Historical/Great Fires|Great Fires]] of 354 [[Nova Aetas|NA]], they were incorporated into the Anachreon as the Syndicate of Timber and Crown and given royal backing to enforce controlled harvests.

Because of its origin in halfling practices, the Syndicate once was a good mix of humans and halflings, which gave them legitimacy with the common folk. However, that mixture has shifted in favor of humans and now the few remaining halflings in leadership are under heavy pressure.
