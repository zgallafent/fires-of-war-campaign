The Order of Chain and Flame, sometimes known by their older name of Ironmongers, is a guild of smiths and armorers in the [[Anachreon (nation)]] and a member guild of the [[Council of Eight]]. They came together after the Great Iron Shortage 130 years ago, where they negotiated on behalf of the consortia in the Cindervale Mountains to supply the crown's armies with steel.

They are associated with the [[Forge Collegium]] in the [[Arcaneum]].

The guild generally handles military contracts for the Anachreon and have a lot of influence with the generals. They often clash with the [[Bastion Collegium of Master Engineers]] over siege equipment patents.
