The Council of Eight, also known as the Concord or the Eightfold, is a congress of the major guild-colleges in the [[Anachreon (nation)]]. Each group sends their Guildmaster, an [[Arcaneum|Arcaneum]]-appointed Provost, and a chosen delegate from the guild ranks. These 24 members meet in the Hall of Concord at the heart of the Arcaneum to rule on tariffs, supply chains, and much of the Arcaneum's direction of study. Many consider the Concord to be the true authority in the Anachreon.

The Concord is composed of eight guild-colleges:
- The [[Bastion Collegium of Master Engineers]] and the [[Bastionum]]
- The [[Collegium of Veilwrights and Mantlers]] and the [[Chromatic Cloisters]]
- The [[Conclave of the Amberglass]] and the [[Prismatica]]
- The [[Concordant Fraternity of Riverwardens]] and the [[Thalassic Wing]]
- The [[League of Salt and Silver]] and the [[Vaulted Halls]]
- The [[Order of Chain and Flame]] and the [[Forge Collegium]]
- The [[Syndicate of Timber and Crown]] and the [[Verdant Hall]]
- The [[Tribunal of the Golden Sheaf]] and the [[Granary Sanctum]]
These groups have long been in a struggle for power, which often cripples the decision-making of the Concord. The main conflict are over:
- Invention credits (Engineers and Ironmongers)
- Food prices (League and Tribunal)
- Roads and bridges (Riverwardens and Engineers)
- Land use (Syndicate and Tribunal)
